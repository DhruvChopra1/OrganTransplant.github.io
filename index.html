What is Organ Donation?

Organ donation is a life-saving process where organs from one person are transplanted into another to save their life. The most common types of organ donations are kidney, heart, liver, and lung transplants. Donors can be living or deceased, and organ transplants provide a second chance at life for individuals suffering from organ failure.
