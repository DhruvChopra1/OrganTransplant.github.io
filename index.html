What is Organ Donation?

Organ donation is the process of giving an organ or tissue to someone in need of a transplant. It can save lives and improve the quality of life for people suffering from organ failure.

Facts About Organ Donation

One Donor Can Save Up to 8 Lives

Organs from a single donor can help save or improve the lives of 8 people.

Thousands Are Waiting

There are currently thousands of people on the organ transplant list, waiting for a life-saving donation.

It's a Simple Process

Registering as an organ donor is simple and could make a huge difference.

The Organ Donation Process

1. Register as an organ donor.

2. When you pass away, medical professionals will check if your organs are suitable for donation.

3. If suitable, the organs will be transplanted to those in need of a life-saving operation.
