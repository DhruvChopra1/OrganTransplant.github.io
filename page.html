About Organ Transplant Technology

Understanding the science and process of organ transplantation

What is an organ transplant?

An organ transplant is when a donor gives their healthy organ to somebody who has an organ beyond healing. Some examples of commonly transplanted organs include the liver, kidney, heart, and lungs.

The Process of Organ Transplantation

Successful transplantation requires a suitable donor match. Doctors evaluate blood type, organ size, and overall health before prioritizing patients in need.

How Does it Work?

The process involves removing a damaged organ and replacing it with a functioning organ from a donor, ensuring continued bodily function.

What Is to be expected?

After surgery, patients undergo intensive monitoring, medication, and regular checkups to ensure the transplanted organ functions properly.

More on Why.

Organ transplantation saves lives. In 2022 alone, over 40,000 organ transplants were performed in the USA, giving recipients a second chance at life.
